The Industry Problem

High energy bills. Low production output.
The gap is worker negligence — and it's invisible.

In most garment and textile factories, managers have no way to see what's happening at the machine level — until the monthly bill arrives. By then, the damage is done.

🔌

Machines Running, Nobody Working

Sewing lines left powered on during breaks, lunch, and handover. Each idle line consumes full standby power — multiplied by dozens of lines across a shift.

MQTT Sensor Real-Time

Production Below Target, No Alert

Utilities Left On After Hours

Lights, HVAC, compressors, and auxiliary equipment running overnight or on weekends. A single overlooked switch can cost thousands in a month.

How Technurah Works

From factory floor to manager's screen
in under 60 seconds

01

Install IoT Smart Sensors

Clip-on IoT energy meters attach to each machine, line, and circuit panel in under 2 hours — no wiring, no shutdown required. ESP32 nodes go live instantly over MQTT.

02

Track Energy + Production Together

Technurah pulls energy data (kW per minute) alongside production counts for every line, every shift, every worker — giving you the energy-to-output ratio that matters.

03

Get Instant Accountability Alerts

The moment a line consumes power without producing output, managers receive an alert with the exact line, time, and energy wasted — enabling immediate corrective action.
